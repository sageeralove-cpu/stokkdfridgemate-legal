Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone, find Stokkd Fridge Mate, and tap Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data is never deleted. Your account reverts to the Free tier, which limits fridge tracking to 15 items and pantry to 5 items. Excess items are preserved but hidden until you reduce below the limit or resubscribe.
Why isn't AI Chef responding?
AI Chef requires an active internet connection. If the AI service is temporarily unavailable, the app will automatically show local recipe suggestions instead. Check your connection and try again. If the issue persists, contact us.
I've hit my monthly usage cap. What now?
Usage caps reset on the 1st of each month. You can continue using all non-AI features (fridge tracking, expiry alerts, shopping lists) while waiting for the reset, or upgrade to the Family plan for higher limits.
Are AI-generated recipes safe for allergies?
AI Chef provides recipe suggestions based on your fridge contents, but may not identify all potential allergens. If you have food allergies or sensitivities, always independently verify all ingredients before preparing any recipe.
How do I restore my subscription on a new device?
Open the app on your new device and tap "Restore Purchases" on the pricing screen. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
Can I share my Family plan with others?
Yes! The Family plan supports up to 6 accounts. Family members can be invited from within the app's settings after subscribing.

Bug Reports

When reporting a bug, please include: a description of what happened, what you expected to happen, your iPhone model and iOS version, and a screenshot if possible. This helps us resolve issues faster.
