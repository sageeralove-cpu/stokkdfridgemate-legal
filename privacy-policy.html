Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Stokkd Fridge Mate ("the App") is operated by Jax ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name and email address for authentication and communication purposes.

App Data: Fridge and pantry inventory (item names, quantities, expiry dates), dietary preferences, shopping lists, and meal plans. This data is stored to provide the core functionality of the App.

Usage Data: Anonymised analytics including feature usage frequency, session duration, and crash reports to help us improve the App.

Subscription Data: Your subscription status (Free, Pro, or Family) is managed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store payment information.

2. AI Features and Data Processing

Pro and Family subscribers have access to AI Chef, powered by Anthropic's Claude AI. Understanding how your data is used with AI is important to us.

What is sent to AI: When you use AI Chef, the following information is sent to our secure server to generate personalised recipe suggestions: your fridge and pantry contents (item names, expiry status, stock levels), your dietary preferences, and your conversation with AI Chef within the current session.

What is NOT sent to AI: Your name, email address, location, device information, or any other personal identifiers are never included in AI requests.

AI Processing: AI requests are processed through Anthropic's Claude API via our secure server. Your API key and all server-side credentials are never exposed to the App or your device. Anthropic processes data in accordance with their privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

Data Retention: AI conversation history is stored only in your current app session and is not persisted on our servers. Each AI request is stateless from the server's perspective.

3. Fallback Logic

If the AI service is temporarily unavailable due to server maintenance, network issues, or service interruptions, the App automatically falls back to built-in local recipe suggestions. Local fallback uses only on-device data and does not transmit any information externally. Local suggestions may be less personalised than AI-generated ones.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: provide and maintain the App's core features (fridge tracking, expiry alerts, recipe suggestions); generate AI-powered recipe recommendations for subscribers; improve the App through anonymised usage analytics; communicate important updates about the service.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share data only with: Anthropic (for AI recipe generation, Pro/Family subscribers only, as described above); Apple (for subscription management via the App Store); analytics providers (anonymised data only, no personal identifiers).

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), secure server infrastructure hosted on Vercel, and API key management that ensures credentials are never exposed client-side.

7. Data Storage and Location

Your fridge and pantry data is stored locally on your device. AI requests are processed through servers that may be located outside Australia. By using AI features, you consent to this data processing.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to: access your personal data by contacting us; request deletion of your account and associated data; opt out of AI features by using the Free tier; export your fridge and pantry data.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
