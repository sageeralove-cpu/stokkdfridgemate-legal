Section 3

AI Features & Third-Party Services

Anthropic (Claude AI) — Recipe generation, food detection & waste prediction

When you use AI features, FridgeMate sends your food item names and expiry dates to Anthropic's Claude API. No personal information (name, email, location) is included. Anthropic does not retain API request data beyond the duration of the request. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.

Open Food Facts — Barcode scanner

When you scan a barcode, the barcode number is sent to the Open Food Facts public API to look up product information. No personal data is transmitted. Open Food Facts is a free, open-source food database. Their privacy policy is at openfoodfacts.org/privacy.

Apple (Sign In, Subscriptions & StoreKit)

If you sign in with Apple, your authentication is managed entirely by Apple. We receive only a unique user identifier and optionally your email (which Apple may relay or hide). Subscription payments are processed exclusively by Apple — we never see your payment card details. Apple's privacy policy is at apple.com/privacy.

Google (Sign In with Google)

If you choose to sign in with Google, authentication is managed by Google OAuth. We receive your name and email address only. Google's privacy policy is at policies.google.com/privacy.

⚠️ AI feature opt-out

You can disable all AI features at any time in Settings → AI & Features → AI Recipe Generation. When disabled, no data is sent to Anthropic.

Section 4

How We Use Your Information

We use information only for the following purposes:

App functionality

Tracking your fridge and pantry contents, generating expiry alerts, producing AI recipe suggestions, and enabling shopping and meal planning features.

Account management

Authenticating you, syncing your data across your devices, and managing your subscription status.

App improvement

Anonymous, aggregated analytics (only collected if you grant App Tracking Transparency permission) to understand which features are used and improve them.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose not listed above. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

Section 5

Data Storage & Security

Your fridge, pantry, shopping, and meal plan data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage. If you create an account, your data may additionally be synchronised to our secure cloud database (Supabase, hosted in the EU/US) to enable multi-device access.

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including encrypted transit (HTTPS/TLS), encrypted storage at rest, and access controls. However, no system is 100% secure — please use a strong password and keep your device updated.

Camera frames captured for AI food detection are processed in real time and are never stored on our servers or on your device beyond the immediate analysis.
